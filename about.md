Hey there, I'm Willard. 

Welcome to my digital garden. Unlike a traditional blog, this space is meant to be a collection of evolving thoughts, notes, and essays. Some are polished, while others are just seeds of ideas.

### What I Do

I enjoy working at the intersection of technology and creativity. Whether I'm building a new side project, exploring the bleeding edge of web development, or just trying to optimize my daily workflows, I usually write about it here to help clarify my own thinking.

### My Interests

- **Tech & Programming**: Exploring new languages, frameworks, and tools.
- **Reading & Writing**: Consuming good books and trying to articulate interesting ideas.
- **Life & Philosophy**: Pondering the human experience and how to live a good life.
- **Journaling**: Keeping a log of the day-to-day.

Feel free to explore the notes. You might find something interesting!
